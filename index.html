Empowering the Visually Impaired with AI

Suradas is a revolutionary AI-powered wearable designed to assist visually impaired individuals by providing real-time navigation, object recognition, and voice-guided help. With built-in sensors, AI processing, and smart connectivity, Suradas enables greater independence and mobility in daily life.

Why Suradas?

  • AI-based object & human detection
  • Voice-activated navigation
  • Live GPS tracking and emergency alerts
  • Affordable and scalable design
  • Designed for accessibility, comfort, and reliability
