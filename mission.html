Vision

To establish Suradas as a global leader in assistive technology, setting new benchmarks in accessibility, inclusivity, and innovation for the visually impaired community.

Core Values

  • Innovation: Harnessing the latest in AI and sensor technologies.
  • Accessibility: Ensuring affordability and usability for all.
  • Independence: Enhancing self-reliance through real-time assistance.
  • Inclusivity: Designing with empathy and user needs at the center.
  • Safety: Integrating emergency features and navigation support.

Impact Goals

Suradas aims to reach millions of visually impaired individuals globally, starting with a pilot rollout in India and expanding to regions in North America, Europe, and Southeast Asia. Our focus is not only on product innovation but on creating lasting social impact through partnerships with NGOs, educators, and healthcare providers.
